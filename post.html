What is True Enlightenment?

Enlightenment is not a destination you arrive at, but the realization of where you have always been. We spend lifetimes searching for peace, believing it exists in the next accomplishment, the next relationship, or the next moment.

Many traditions speak of awakening. In Zen, it is Satori. In Yoga, it is Samadhi. But at its core, it is simply the dropping away of the illusion of separation.

"The moment you realize you are not present, you are present. Whenever you are able to observe your mind, you are no longer trapped in it."

When you sit in silence, what remains? The thoughts come and go like clouds in the sky, but you are not the clouds. You are the sky itself. Vast, untroubled, and infinitely still.

In future posts, we will explore practical ways to integrate this stillness into our chaotic daily lives.
